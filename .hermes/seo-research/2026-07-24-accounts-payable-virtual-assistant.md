## Why this keyword

Ahrefs shows mixed job and service intent, but the query itself names the role a buyer may hire. The current site has planning, first-task, provider-question, and onboarding-checklist articles, but it does not have a role guide for an accounts payable virtual assistant. A role-scope article can support the managed staffing offer without replacing the homepage or any service route.

The H1 will begin with the exact keyword: `Accounts payable virtual assistant: tasks, controls, and hiring plan`.

## Content gap and intent plan

The commercial pages tend to sell the role, while job boards pull the SERP toward employment intent. The article should make buyer intent clear in the title, opening answer, task table, controls, first-week plan, scripts, and managed staffing CTA. It should not copy the job-board angle or publish pay rates.

The site already covers general outsourcing planning, first tasks, provider questions, and onboarding. This article fills the narrower role-definition gap: what an accounts payable virtual assistant can prepare, what must stay with the finance owner, how to set access, and how to review the first 30 days.
